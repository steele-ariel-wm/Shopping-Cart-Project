I've ordered baked goods and haven't received them.

Desserts Box takes full responsibility for dispersing your sweet treats as scheduled, but is
not responsible for packages once they have left our facility.


How can I track my baked goods?

You will receive an email confirmation when your package leaves our facility which will
include a tracking number for you to track the location of your package.


How do you ship your refrigerated goods?

Our cream-based items are packaged with dry ice for national shipping to ensure freshness! Make
sure to place your cream-based products directly in the freezer upon arrival to ensure freshness.


How do I cancel an order?

All food sales are final.


Is Desserts Box gluten free?

Desserts Box is not currently gluten free.


Is Desserts Box nut free?

Desserts Box is not nut free, we have certain desserts that are specific to have nuts in them.


Can I purchase specialty designed cakes?

Desserts Box currently does not make specialty cakes.


Does Desserts Box ship nationwide?

Desserts Box only sells in the US currently. We are working on shipping nationwide.
